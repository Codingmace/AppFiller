<p><strong>Security Architect (L3) - Technology Consulting: Security and Privacy</strong></p>
<p><strong>Description</strong><br><br>As a Security Architect in Crederas Security and Privacy capability group, you will provide Security leadership for multiple Credera clients and guide consultant teams on end-to-end delivery. You'll play an integral role in helping our clients ensure they are protected by developing solutions focused on security, efficiently integrate and manage new or existing technology systems to deliver continuous operational improvements, increase their cybersecurity investment, and detect, respond, and remediate threats.</p>
<p>On a typical day, you might expect to participate in any of the following:</p>
<ul>
<li>Serve as a subject matter expert in Information Security on projects and continuously interact with stakeholders</li>
<li>Communicate the business benefits of improved cybersecurity to clients and leaders across an organization</li>
<li>Lead Cloud Security maturity assessment identifying key gaps from client's maturity levels</li>
<li>Lead the definition of Crederas Cybersecurity methods and patterns of practice</li>
<li>Lead the development and publication of Cybersecurity perspectives and opinions for Credera</li>
<li>Facilitate executive-level workshops</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>WHO YOU ARE:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>You have at least 6 years of hands-on experience in the Cybersecurity industry or with technology consulting</li>
<li>You have a passion for cybersecurity and keep yourself up to date on the latest developments in the and trends</li>
<li>You have proven technical skills including familiarity or direct technical experience with Identity and Access Management, Data Protection and Vulnerability Management technologies</li>
<li>You have experience with at least 1 of the following: Amazon Web Services, Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud Platform</li>
<li>You have experience performing competitive and best practice assessments and researching activities to identify competitive benchmarks</li>
<li>You have experience performing technical and competitive analysis of risk, controls, third party management, security operations, privacy and data protection solutions, including integration with enterprise information security and information technology applications and data feeds;</li>
<li>You are a subject matter expertise in information security and risk management</li>
<li>You hold security certifications such as Certified Information Security Manager (CISM), Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP), Certified Ethical Hack (CEH), or Certified Cloud Security Professional (CCSP) desired</li>
<li>You can lead cross-functional teams, develop ideas to bring together brand goals, and tailor objectives towards consumer needs</li>
<li>You have strong communication and interpersonal skills and the ability to engage customers at a business level in addition to a technical level. You can handle complex organizational dynamics to deliver a solution to a clients satisfaction</li>
<li>You are accomplished at mentoring, leading, and developing others</li>
<li>You can drive business development and are comfortable with developing business cases and justifications that grow the customer relationship and satisfaction</li>
<li>You can create and deliver security processes and provide leadership with minimal guidance</li>
</ul>
